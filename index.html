COOPERATIVE COMMERCE

mycoopshop.com is a "Hypermarket" or "online mall". We provide sole-traders & small businesses the ability to sell domestically or export from an online marketplace. Our Coop helps businesses establish an online shop front with their own products and services or build a shop through a distributor to list products for them; Our Coop is the flagship online store increasing each businesses overall profitability through reduced business expense. By cost sharing and cooperative marketing, we increase profit without having to increase the price.
